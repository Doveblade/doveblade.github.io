Anushka works on cyber-physical security across networks, OT/ICS and embedded systems, with a focus on RF environments, robotics, and networked autonomous platforms.

Her research spans offensive security and autonomous systems architecture - adversarial analysis on industrial protocols, RF signal exploitation, and edge-autonomous stacks operating in contested environments, with system architecture informed by biological first principles.

Her methodology integrates zero-trust and secure-by-design frameworks with threat modelling, digital twin simulation, and penetration testing.
